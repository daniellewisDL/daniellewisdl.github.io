Five equations that changed the world

Interactive explorers for five of the equations from Ian Stewart’s 2012 book In Pursuit of the Unknown. Pull the sliders and watch each one come alive. Originally a Streamlit app — rebuilt here in D3.

Equation selection from Ian Stewart, In Pursuit of the Unknown: 17 Equations That Changed the World (2012). Explanatory text adapted from Wikipedia. Black–Scholes prices and Greeks computed client-side with a normal-CDF approximation; all figures are illustrative, not investment advice. · Rebuilt from a Streamlit original with D3 + KaTeX by Daniel Lewis.
