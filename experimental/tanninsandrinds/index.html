Artisanal Vegan Cheeses & Curated Wines

Our Philosophy

At Tannins & Rinds, we believe that luxury and compassion can coexist beautifully. We are dedicated to the art of plant-based cheesemaking, creating complex, flavourful, and utterly convincing vegan cheeses that honour traditional methods.

Each wheel is aged with care, developing nuanced rinds and deep character. We pair our creations with a meticulously curated selection of vegan wines, chosen to complement and elevate the tasting experience. This is a new chapter in opulence—crafted by nature, perfected by passion.

The Collection

Smoked Cashew Gouda

A firm, smoky cheese with a creamy finish. Aged for 60 days and cold-smoked over hickory wood. Pairs wonderfully with a bold Syrah.

Herbed Almond Chèvre

A soft, tangy, and spreadable cheese rolled in fresh herbs de Provence. Bright and zesty, perfect with a crisp Sauvignon Blanc.

Truffle & Macadamia Camembert

An indulgent, bloomy-rind cheese with a rich, buttery texture and an earthy black truffle aroma. A true delight with a glass of Pinot Noir.

Private Tastings

Join us for an intimate and guided exploration of our finest pairings. Our private tasting events are an opportunity to deepen your appreciation for the craft of vegan cheese and wine. Hosted in our cellar tasting room, it is an experience of pure indulgence.
